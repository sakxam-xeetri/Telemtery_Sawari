About Sawari

Empowering seamless urban mobility

Getting around Kathmandu by bus shouldn't involve guesswork. We built Sawari to bridge the gap between complex transit networks and everyday commuters. By leveraging real-time data and community insights, we provide a sophisticated, app-free platform designed specifically for the unique rhythm of the Valley.

  • Hyper-Local Knowledge: Understands local route names, stop landmarks, and transit patterns naturally.
  • Frictionless Access: Works instantly in your browser with no downloads or sign-ups required.
  • Community Powered: Rider feedback continuously updates routes and stops, keeping data accurate for everyone.

How It Works

Four steps to your destination

From opening the app to catching your bus — it's simple and fast.

  • 1

    Tell us where you're going

    Type your destination in plain language — "Ratnapark to Lagankhel" or drop a pin on the map.

  • 2

    Explore route options

    See direct buses and smart transfer combos ranked by speed and convenience.

  • 3

    Know your fare upfront

    Get fare estimates based on official DoTM tariff rates — no surprises at the conductor's window.

  • 4

    Catch your bus

    Live tracking shows approaching buses so you can time your arrival at the stop perfectly.
